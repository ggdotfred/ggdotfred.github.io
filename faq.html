Frequently Asked Questions

What is BenchBug?

BenchBug is a platform to discover all the trending topics on health and fitness. Users like you provide all of the content where you can "like" and "comment" on posts.


I want to change my username. Do I have to start a new account?

Yes. Once a user account is created, the username cannot be edited.


I made a mistake with my post, how can I edit it?

Categories and images cannot be edited but post descriptions can be edited. If you want, you can simply delete the post and create a new one again.


Am I allowed to advertise on BenchBug?

Unless approved, posts that have explicit advertising will be treated as spam and be promptly removed. Further violations will lead to the user being banned from the BenchBug app.

ToastBeans will be available on iOS. We are looking into expanding into other platforms in the future!

We're sorry for the inconvenience! Please send them to toastbeansapp@gmail.com with the subject: App Bug.

Thank you for your suggestion! Please send them to toastbeansapp@gmail.com with the subject: Feature Request.
